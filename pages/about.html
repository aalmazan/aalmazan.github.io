This blog

After multiple iterations, failed experiments, and crushed dreams of having an over-engineered content management and/or blog system just for this site, I ended up with what you see now.

This site uses Pelican as its static site generator.

The theme templates are loosely based on the wonderful Cid Pelican theme (preview) with styling help (aka a little copy-pasta) from Tigran Hakobyan's blog.

Base styling provided by Tailwind CSS, @tailwind/typography, and daisyUI with the dracula theme.

Font used are Gabarito and Roboto Slab.
